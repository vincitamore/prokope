# Lens: humble-pole

Relocate the problem so the powerful or infinite sits at the humble or origin pole — change of chart, not mere reduction of load.

## Questions that fire it

- What frame makes the hard object sit at a simple coordinate or boundary?
- Are we under-engineering (dropping load) while claiming humility, or truly relocating fullness?
- What becomes easy to compute or prove only after the relocation — name that payoff?
