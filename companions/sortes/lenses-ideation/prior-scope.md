# Lens: prior-scope

Closed priors kill specific attack shapes, not whole quests. Name the shape that died and the remainder still live.

## Questions that fire it

- What claim, under which definitions, was actually refuted — and what neighboring claims share none of those definitions?
- Which “we already tried that” stories are re-runs of the same shape with new nouns?
- What would count as a *new* shape rather than a rename of the closed one?
