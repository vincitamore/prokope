# Lens: warrant-scale

Match claim strength to exhibit strength — diagnostic, intermediate result, substrate lemma, or declared end-state win are different scales.

## Questions that fire it

- What is the strongest claim the *current* exhibit licenses without metaphor or packaging upgrade?
- Are we using language from a higher scale (full proof, campaign win, production-ready) for a lower-scale result?
- What additional exhibit would be required to climb exactly one scale — named, not vague “more work”?
