# Lens: multiplicative-bridge

Geometry, duality, or labels alone do not yield an algebra of composition; seek an explicit bridge from the current objects to a composition law (product, convolution, monoid action, tensor, or parallel composition).

## Questions that fire it

- What operation on the working objects would correspond to composition / product / convolution — even approximately — and is it first-class or only metaphorical?
- Does inversion, dual, or transport *commute*, fail to commute, or obstruct that composition law — and is that measured?
- Can a generating-product, series, or monoid identity be stated as an operator on the working space without pure transport of a classical formula?
