# Lens: invariance-class

Name the symmetries, reorderings, or equivalences that leave a proposed “signal” unchanged — if the signal is invariant under them, it cannot be essential to that action.

## Questions that fire it

- Under what transformations (reorder, relabel, conjugate, shuffle of equivalent inputs, dual indexing) does the measured effect stay the same?
- If the effect is invariant under an action we claimed was essential, is the claim structurally blocked rather than merely unlucky?
- What refined statistic would break the invariance class and become a real discriminator?
