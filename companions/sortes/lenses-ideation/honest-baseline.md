# Lens: honest-baseline

Advantage claims die or live against the *relevant* competitor, not a straw under-implementation.

## Questions that fire it

- What is the strongest classical or prior method in the *same generator class* — and is that what we compare to?
- Would the reported win reverse if the baseline gained the same cheap filters, tables, or pre-processing we used?
- Is wall-clock, asymptotic, or query-count the right bar for this claim class — and did we pre-register it?
