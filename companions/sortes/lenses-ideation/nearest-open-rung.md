# Lens: nearest-open-rung

Between exercises and the summit there is a ladder of genuinely open questions. Locate your object in the live literature and aim at the lowest rung nobody has climbed — real, claimable territory that is a step toward the summit, never a substitute for it.

## Questions that fire it

- What is the nearest question about your exact object that is actually open — not classical-and-solved (relabeling wins nothing) and not the summit itself?
- Who last moved on that question, with what tool, and what specifically stopped them — does your substrate supply anything they lacked?
- If your current thread succeeded completely, would the result be new to the field or only new to this campaign — and what smallest strengthening would flip it to the former?

## Overfire (vice)

- Rung-collecting: accumulating small open questions without keeping the summit in view.
- Mistaking "hard for us today" for "open" — the frontier check is a literature act, not an introspection.
